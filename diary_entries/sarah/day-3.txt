END OF DAY — SARAH'S REFLECTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Sales were lower than Monday and Tuesday but the day was productive
  in less visible ways. The accountant call, the BID meeting, the bank
  visit — none of these ring the till but all of them keep the business
  functioning. The things that feel like not-work are often the most
  important work.

  The author share of Instagram is the best thing that happened today.
  When someone with 12,000 followers shares your content, that's worth
  more than any ad spend.

  I'm behind on the window display but I'll do it tomorrow and it'll
  be worth it. The mid-week window is when office workers stop and look
  on their lunch breaks.

  Financial state of mind: cautious but stable. The corporate invoice
  situation stressed me more than it should have. Cashflow anxiety
  is the silent tax on small business ownership.
