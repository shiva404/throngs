09:20 AM - 10:00 AM  [40 min] VOID PREPARATION

  I closed QB. I opened the QB help article: "Void or delete transactions in
  QuickBooks Online." Read it once, slowly.

  Key things from the article:
    - To void an invoice: open the invoice, click "More" at the bottom,
      select "Void."
    - QB will warn you before voiding: "Are you sure you want to void this
      transaction? This will zero out the amounts."
    - The invoice will remain in QB as a record, stamped "VOID."
    - You cannot un-void an invoice (you'd have to create a new one).
    - Voiding affects any attached journal entries — but since this invoice
      has no payments attached, Raj confirmed, there should be nothing else.

  Read it a second time. Took notes in my notepad word for word.

  Then I thought of a question: if I void an invoice that's already been

  Searched QB help: "does voiding an invoice notify the customer?" The help

10:55 AM - 11:20 AM  [25 min] CHEAT SHEET UPDATE — VOID PROCEDURE

  Added a new page to my cheat sheet. This is one of the most detailed pages
  because I want to capture every step and caution:

  ═══════════════════════════════
  HOW TO VOID AN INVOICE IN QB
  (ONLY DO THIS WITH RAJ'S AUTHORIZATION)
  ═══════════════════════════════
  1. Get written (email/message) authorization from Raj first.
  2. Sales > Invoices > find the invoice by INV number.
  3. Before doing ANYTHING: verify you are looking at the correct invoice:
     - Invoice number matches what Raj specified
     - Customer name is correct
     - Amount matches what Raj described
     - Status: should be unpaid/sent (if a payment has been applied,
       STOP and ask Raj — this is more complex)
     - Activity log: confirm no payment recorded
  4. If all clear: get Raj to visually confirm (walk over if possible).
  5. Scroll to bottom of invoice > click "More" > click "Void"
     (DO NOT click "Delete" — Delete is permanent and removes the record)
  6. Read the confirmation modal carefully.
  7. Click "Yes" to confirm.
  8. Verify invoice now shows $0.00 and status "VOID."
  9. Screenshot the voided invoice.
  10. Print a copy for the AR binder.
  11. Log in Excel: INV number, customer, amount, VOIDED date, authorized by.
  12. Tell Raj it's done.

  NOTE: Voiding does NOT notify the customer automatically.
  NOTE: A void cannot be undone. Be 100% certain before clicking.
