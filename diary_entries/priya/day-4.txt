09:15 AM - 09:40 AM  [25 min] RAJ CONSULTATION — PARTIAL PAYMENT

  Went to Raj's desk at 9:16. He pulled out a chair and we went through it.

  Raj's explanation:
    "Partial payments happen sometimes. Hartwell probably has a cash flow
     thing or they're paying off a chunk to stay current. Here's what you do:
     record the $3,100 against INV-2849, same way you'd record any payment.
     QB will apply it and leave INV-2849 open with a remaining balance of
     $3,002. The invoice will show as 'Partial' in the system. When the rest
     of the payment comes in — probably before the Apr 7 due date — you
     record that too and it'll close out."

  Me: "What if they only ever pay $3,100 and not the full amount?"
  Raj: "Then after the due date we'd have a problem. But don't worry about
       that now. Apply the partial, note it in the system, and we'll watch
       it as we get closer to Apr 7."

  Me: "Should I send Hartwell any kind of confirmation or acknowledgement
      that we received the partial?"
  Raj: "No, we don't typically do that for partials. They know what they paid.
       If anything, let me know when it's recorded and I'll note it on my end."

  Me: "One more thing — the check is dated 03/11 but it arrived today.
      Do I use the check date or today's date?"
  Raj: "Use the check date. 03/11."
  Me: "Got it."

  I wrote all of this down on my notepad in real time. Raj is patient about
  me taking notes during these conversations. He never makes me feel dumb for
  asking. I know he's busy but he's never made me feel like a burden.

01:00 PM - 03:00 PM  [120 min] PROCESS DOC + CHEAT SHEET + ADMIN

  Updated cheat sheet with a new page for Partial Payments:
    - Go to Receive Payment for the customer (same path as full payment)
    - Enter the check date (NOT today's date if check is older)
    - Enter check number from physical check
    - Enter amount received (the actual check amount)
    - QB will auto-apply to oldest invoice — VERIFY which invoice it's being
      applied to before saving
    - Verify: Amount to credit = $0.00 and Difference = $0.00 before saving
    - Invoice will show status "Partial" with remaining balance
