08:20 AM - 08:55 AM  [35 min] QB ONLINE — SUNSET HARDWARE (SESSION 1)

  ⬛ QUICKBOOKS ONLINE SESSION 1  [08:20 AM – 08:55 AM, 35 min]

  Logged into QBO. Switched company to Sunset Hardware. And immediately
  noticed something wrong.

  The left navigation bar looked different. Yesterday when I worked on Bella's,
  the menu items along the left side were: Dashboard, Banking, Sales, Expenses,
  Employees, Reports, Taxes, Accounting, My Accountant. Today — same browser,
  same account — it shows: Dashboard, Transactions, Sales, Expenses,
  Payroll, Reports, Taxes, Accounting, My Accountant.

  "Banking" became "Transactions." And "Employees" became "Payroll."

  QBO updated overnight. I know they do this — they have a banner sometimes
  that says "We've made improvements!" — but they don't warn you that the
  menu words changed. I sat there for a full minute trying to figure out if
  I was in the right company file.

  I searched my notepad where I write navigation paths. "Banking > Bank
  Accounts" was my note. Clicked "Transactions." The bank accounts were there.
  So it's the same screen, just renamed. Fine. But I had to relearn where it
  was in my memory. Annoying.

  Also: the "Accounting" menu used to say "Chart of Accounts" at the top when
  I clicked it. Today it shows "Reconcile" first and "Chart of Accounts"
  second. The order changed. Small thing, I know. But when you're working
  from muscle memory, small things matter.

  Wrote in my notepad: NEW menu: Transactions (was Banking). Payroll (was
  Employees). Gear > Your Company > Chart of Accounts — SAME. Reconcile is
  under Accounting, still there.

  Navigated to the AR to check overdue invoices. Sales > Invoices. The list
  loaded. I sorted by "Due Date" to get the oldest first. The three overdue
  invoices were right at the top. Good.

  I needed to add an aging note to each — something I do in Sunset Hardware's
  file as a reminder when Dennis reviews with me. In Desktop, I could add a
  memo right on the invoice record. In QBO... clicked into Invoice #1047.
  There's a "Message on invoice" field at the bottom and a "Memo" field. The
  Memo field said it was for internal use. I added: "74 days past due as of
  4/1 — follow up with school district billing dept." Saved.

  Did the same for #1052 and #1058 (Harmon Construction). Added notes.

  Then I noticed something: Invoice #1052 shows an amount of $1,200.00 but in
  my paper file I had noted $1,250.00 from last month's printout. $50 off.
